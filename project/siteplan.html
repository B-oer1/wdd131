Structured Infrastructure Plan for Scaling Scripture Engagement

Strategic Overview

Site Name

Disciples Word Club

Chosen to represent an intentional global community centered entirely on regular, high-density scripture intake.

Site Purpose

To provide believers globally with structural frameworks, tracking tools, and social accountability mechanics necessary to finish reading the complete Bible within targeted yearly horizons.

User Scenarios

  • Scenario 1:

    "What is the most preferred structured reading plan for a busy schedule?"

  • Scenario 2:

    "How can I access my custom Bible tracker and view real-time metrics?"

  • Scenario 3:

    "Who follows up on me or keeps me accountable when I begin lagging behind?"
